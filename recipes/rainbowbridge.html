Description

Bifrost is a burning rainbow bridge that reaches between Midgard (Earth) and Asgard, the realm of the gods.

Ingredients

  • Nuclear Glass
  • Pot of Rainbow
  • Cosmic Coordinates

Steps

  1. Fuse nuclear glass to cause a giant explosion
  2. Dump the pot of rainbow onto the nuclear hot molten glass and let it flow
  3. Attach it to the Cosmic coordinates
